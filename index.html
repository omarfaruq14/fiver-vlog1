5 Ways Businesses Can Use Aerial Drone Photography

by fiver team | August 3, 2023

While drone photography might once have been an expensive luxury reserved for the uber-techy, today, it's becoming a must-have asset for businesses in various industries. By providing a safe and effective way to capture aerial videos and still overhead photos, drone technology is making a major impact on everything from sales and marketing to safety and security.

Here's a look at some of the ways drones are transforming the ways business is done in several key industries.

1. Real Estate

Finding an excellent professional photographer has long been a critical part of real estate marketing, and while beautiful interior photos still go a long way toward making the sale, aerial photos and videos give potential buyers a whole new perspective.

Drone photos provide up-to-date images of properties, giving buyers a much better look at the location and surrounding area. While commercial real estate agents have been using aerial photography for quite some time, the ease and cost-effectiveness of using drones have made this accessible for all types of real estate marketing, essentially revolutionizing the residential real estate marketplace.

2. Construction

Construction sites are often large and sometimes difficult to access. Drone photography allows construction firms to provide clients with regularly scheduled photo and/or video updates of their progress. The images captured can streamline project timelines, assist with inventory management, and improve site organization.

High-resolution drone images can be turned into 3D models, helping construction crews identify challenges during the pre-construction planning phase. Overhead drone images can also help with tracking equipment and remotely troubleshooting malfunctions.
